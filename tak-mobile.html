Tak (Mobile)

Tak is a two-player abstract strategy game designed by James Ernest and Patrick Rothfuss.

I'm currently working on this mobile rendition of the game using Unity.


Current features:

- Create a game, invite another player in your contacts to play it with you.
- Join another player's game after receiving an invite from them.
- Create a quick match with a random player.
- Select from 3x3, 4x4, 5x5, 6x6, and 8x8 game board modes.
- Pinch to zoom game area, swipe to rotate.
- Place stones, either as regular or standing stones.
- Move stones around the board, onto other stones, and move stacks of stones.
- Limitations for stone placement and movements based on original game rules.


Features currently in development:

- Player rules and stopping movement during other player's turn.
- Win conditions, algorithm for checking if conditions are met.
- Notification and score display for players after a match has ended.
- Audio effects, and corresponding sound settings menu.
- Visual cues and effects for UX.
- Capstones and corresponding functionality.
